Robin Ngangom Profile Details

Robin Ngangom, born in 1959 in Imphal, Manipur, is a distinguished bilingual poet and translator writing in English and Manipuri. A professor at North-Eastern Hill University, Shillong, his poetry reflects the socio-political turmoil of Northeast India, interwoven with personal and lyrical themes. His works, including The Desire of Roots and My Invented Land, explore identity, conflict, and nature, earning him accolades like the Katha Award for Translation (1999) and the Udaya Bharati National Award for Poetry (1994).
