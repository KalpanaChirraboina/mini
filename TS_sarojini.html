Personal Life and Children

Sarojini Naidu married Dr. Govindarajulu Naidu in 1898 in an inter-caste marriage. They had five children: Jayasurya, Padmaja, Randheer, Nilawar, and Leelamani. Her daughter Padmaja became a freedom fighter and Governor of West Bengal. Naidu balanced her public roles with a warm family life.
