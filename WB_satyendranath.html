Satyendranath Dutta was a prolific Bengali poet from West Bengal, celebrated for his mastery of rhyme and rhythm in the early 20th century. Known as the 'Rhyme Wizard,' he introduced innovative metrical forms and sonnets to Bengali poetry, blending traditional and Western influences.
