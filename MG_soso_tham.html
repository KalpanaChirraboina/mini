U Soso Tham Profile Details

U Soso Tham, born in 1873 in Sohra, Meghalaya, is celebrated as the poet laureate of Khasi literature. Despite humble beginnings and limited formal education, he pioneered secular Khasi poetry, blending indigenous idioms with English poetic forms. His works, including Ka Duitara Ksiar (1925) and Ki Sngi Barim U Hynniew Trep (1936), reflect his love for nature, patriotism, and Khasi identity, earning him recognition as a literary icon in Meghalaya.
